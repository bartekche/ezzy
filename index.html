Ezzy - Banking made easy

Questions worth asking:

  • How do I view my PIN?
  • My card got stolen! How do I freeze it?
  • How do I send money to a contact?
  • I want to cancel my subscription
  • How do I increase the limit on my card?
  • ... and you can also try variations of these questions!
    •

When you ask a question, the bot will answer with the first step and will wait for you to type "next", "go forward", "please go to the next step" etc.
There can be multiple instructions, so keep going until you reach the final step.
